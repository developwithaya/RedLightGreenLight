Squid Chase: Red Light, Green Light

Built with WebGL and HTML5

Instructions:

1. Use the arrow keys to move your character.

2. Avoid being caught by the robot.

3. If the robot catches you, you lose a life.

4. If you lose all your lives, the game is over.

5. Reach the finish line to win the game.

6. You can switch players, but it will cost you 10s of your time.

7. You have 3 lives and 3 switches.

8. Other players will attempt to freeze you or get you killed.

9. Tip: A lightning bolt gives you a temporary boost in speed.
